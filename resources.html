What to Include in Your Response Letter

Essential Elements

  • Your full name and SSN
  • Notice number and date
  • Tax year in question
  • Clear statement of your position

Supporting Documentation

  • W-2s and 1099s
  • Bank statements
  • Receipts and invoices
  • Correspondence records

Professional Format

  • Business letter format
  • Professional tone
  • Clear explanations
  • Specific requests

Common Audit Issues and Solutions

Income Discrepancies

When the IRS finds unreported income from third-party reports:

  • Verify the income is yours
  • Check for duplicate reporting
  • Provide corrected documentation
  • Explain any legitimate exclusions

Deduction Disputes

When the IRS questions your business deductions:

  • Provide detailed receipts
  • Explain business purpose
  • Show ordinary and necessary test
  • Document business use percentage
